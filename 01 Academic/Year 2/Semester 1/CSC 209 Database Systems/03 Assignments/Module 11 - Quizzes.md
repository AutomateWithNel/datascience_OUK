### Question 1

Which of the following are advantages of using replication as a design strategy in distributed database systems? (Select all that apply)

Options:
A. Faster data retrieval
B. Simplified data consistency management
C. Improved data availability
D. Simplified data consistency management
E. Easier backup and recovery processes

Answer: Improved data availability, Faster data retrieval, Easier backup and recovery processes
