## Test Your Knowledge

### Question 1

What are the belts in a tyre typically made of?

Options:
a. Copper and aluminum
b. Rayon, steel, and fibreglass
c. Plastic and rubber
d. Nylon and polyester

Answer: Rayon, steel, and fibreglass

### Question 2

What is the primary function of a tyre?

Options:
a. To support the vehicle's electrical system
b. To enhance the vehicle's appearance
c. To provide a flexible and durable interface between the vehicle and the road
d. To increase the vehicle's fuel efficiency

Answer: To provide a flexible and durable interface between the vehicle and the road

### Question 3

What is the purpose of the grooves in a tyre?

Options:
a. To increase tyre rigidity
b. To enhance water dispersion and reduce hydroplaning
c. To reduce tyre noise
d. To improve aesthetic appeal

Answer: To enhance water dispersion and reduce hydroplaning

### Question 4

Which component of a tyre provides traction?

Options:
a. Sidewall
b. Belt
c. Tread
d. Inner liner

Answer: Tread

### Question 5

Which type of tyre is designed for use in icy and snowy conditions?

Options:
a. All-season tyres
b. Run-flat tyres
c. Winter tyres
d. Performance tyres

Answer: Winter tyres
