### Question 1
**What does the pd.concat() function do when used with two DataFrames?**

Options:
- a. Joins two DataFrames on a common column
- b. Concatenates two DataFrames along a particular axis
- c. Merges two DataFrames based on index
- d. Stacks two DataFrames vertically and removes duplicates

Answer:
- b. Concatenates two DataFrames along a particular axis

### Question 2
**What does the unstack method do in pandas?**

Options:
- a. Filters rows in a DataFrame based on a condition
- b. Joins two DataFrames on a common key
- c. Converts a DataFrame from a long format to a wide format
- d. Concatenates two DataFrames along a particular axis

Answer:
- c. Converts a DataFrame from a long format to a wide format

### Question 3
**What is a key difference between the join method and the merge function?**

Options:
- a. join automatically removes duplicates, merge does not
- b. join requires common column names, merge does not
- c. join is used for index-based joining, merge is used for column-based joining
- d. join can only be used with a single DataFrame, merge can be used with multiple DataFrames

Answer:
- c. join is used for index-based joining, merge is used for column-based joining

### Question 4
**What is the purpose of the pivot function in pandas?**

Options:
- a. To concatenate DataFrames along a particular axis
- b. To merge two DataFrames on a common key
- c. To reshape data by converting unique values of a column into new columns
- d. To reshape data by converting columns into rows

Answer:
- c. To reshape data by converting unique values of a column into new columns

### Question 5
**What is the purpose of the stack function in pandas?**

Options:
- a. To filter data based on a condition
- b. To merge two DataFrames on a common key
- c. To concatenate DataFrames along a particular axis
- d. To reshape data by converting columns into rows

Answer:
- d. To reshape data by converting columns into rows

### Question 6
**When using pd.concat([df1, df2]) with axis=0, what happens?**

Options:
- a. df1 and df2 are concatenated horizontally
- b. df1 and df2 are reshaped into a pivot table
- c. df1 and df2 are concatenated vertically
- d. df1 and df2 are merged on a common column

Answer:
- c. df1 and df2 are concatenated vertically

### Question 7
**Which method is used to group data in a DataFrame by one or more columns?**

Options:
- a. df.groupby()
- b. df.pivot()
- c. df.join()
- d. df.concat()

Answer:
- a. df.groupby()

### Question 8
**Which method is used to join two DataFrames on a common key, similar to a SQL join?**

Options:
- a. pd.merge()
- b. pd.concat()
- c. df.pivot()
- d. df.append()

Answer:
- a. pd.merge()
