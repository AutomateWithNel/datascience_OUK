### Question 1
**What is the primary characteristic of time series data?**

Answer:
- a. It is recorded over a period of time

### Question 2
**Which Python module is commonly used for handling date and time data?**

Answer:

```text
datetime
```

### Question 3
**What does the today function in the datetime module return?**

Answer:
- b. The current date.

### Question 4
**In the datetime module, the Blank 1 represents Blank 2**

Answer:
- Blank 1: `timedelta`
- Blank 2: The difference between two datetime values

### Question 5
**What does the `%Y` format specifier in the `strftime` method represent?**

Answer:
- d. Four-digit year

### Question 6
**What is the result of calling `ffill()` on a pandas DataFrame?**

Answer:
- c. Propagates the last valid value forward to fill missing data

### Question 7
**In pandas, converting higher frequency data to lower frequency is called?**

Answer:

```text
downsampling
```

### Question 8
**What happens when you pass the `freq='BME'` to `pd.date_range`?**

Answer:
- a. Generates the last business day of each month

### Question 9
**Moving data backward through time in pandas is referred to?**

Answer:

```text
shifting
```

### Question 10
**What is the purpose of resampling in time series data?**

Answer:
- d. To convert a time series from one frequency to another
