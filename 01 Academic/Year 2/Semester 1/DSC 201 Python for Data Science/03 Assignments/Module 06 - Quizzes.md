### Question 1
**What additional information does a violin plot provide compared to a box plot?**

Answer:
- a. It shows the kernel density estimate, providing a more detailed view of the data's distribution.

### Question 2
**What is Matplotlib, and why is it widely used in data visualization?**

Answer:
- d. A Python library for creating visualizations, known for its flexibility and integration with NumPy and Pandas.

### Question 3
**What is one key advantage of using Plotly over Seaborn?**

Answer:
- b. Plotly excels in creating dynamic and interactive plots.

### Question 4
**What is the difference between a histogram and a bar graph?**

Answer:
- c. A histogram shows the distribution of a dataset, while a bar graph compares different categories.

### Question 5
**What is the primary use of an `lmplot` in Seaborn?**

Answer:
- a. To add a regression line to a scatterplot, useful for linear regression analysis.

### Question 6
**What kind of data is best visualized using a heatmap?**

Answer:
- c. Correlation matrix or data in a matrix format.

### Question 7
**When is a line plot more informative than other types of plots?**

Answer:
- a. When displaying trends over time or continuous data.

### Question 8
**When might you choose a swarm plot over a strip plot?**

Answer:
- a. When you have a larger dataset and want to avoid overlapping data points.

### Question 9
**Which advanced visualization technique allows for the visualization of three-dimensional data?**

Answer:
- c. 3D Scatter Plot
